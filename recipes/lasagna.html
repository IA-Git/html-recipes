Description

Mom's special homemade lasagna recipe with meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Meat: This recipe starts with 1 pound of ground meat (1/2 pound ground pork , 1/2 pound lean ground beef).
  • Onion: A diced onion is cooked with the ground meat.
  • Canned tomatoes: You will need a can of tomato sauce and a can of crushed tomatoes.
  • Parsley and garlic: For fresh flavor, chop 2 tablespoons of parsley and crush 1 clove of garlic.
  • Sugar: A pinch of sugar balances the acidity of the tomatoes.
  • Spices and seasonings: This homemade lasagna is seasoned with dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and ground black pepper.
  • Noodles: Lasagna noodles are required. This recipe calls for uncooked, but you can use the oven-ready variety to save time.
  • Eggs: Three eggs make the cheese layer creamy and help bind it together.

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine pork and ground beef in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Add onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, garlic, basil, salt, oregano, and sugar. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer. stirring occasionally, for 30 minutes.
  5. While the sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes.
    Drain and set aside. While the noodles are cooking, preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  6. Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, remaining 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, salt, and pepper in a large bowl combined.
  7. Assemble lasagna: Spread a spoon or two of sauce over the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish just to coat it. Place two layers of noodles over the sauce to cover.
  8. Layer with 1/2 of the cheese mixture, 1/2 of the remaining sauce, and 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers once more using the remaining noodles, cheese mixture, sauce, and mozzarella.
    Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes. Remove the foil and bake until cheese is golden brown, 5 to 10 more minutes.
  10. Remove from the oven and let stand for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
